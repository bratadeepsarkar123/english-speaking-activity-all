17. It is important for us to become tolerant as a society. Tolerance is the foundation of a harmonious and inclusive society. In a diverse world, where people hold different beliefs, cultures, and perspectives, it is essential to cultivate tolerance and respect for one another. By embracing tolerance, we create space for dialogue, understanding, and empathy. It allows us to bridge divides, challenge prejudices, and build connections based on mutual respect. A tolerant society fosters acceptance, celebrates diversity, and upholds the principles of equality and justice. It encourages individuals to learn from one another, appreciate different viewpoints, and work together towards common goals .
